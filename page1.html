SEO Basics

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization and helps search engines understand your website's content and connect it with users by delivering relevant, valuable results based on their search queries.

Important Factors in SEO

  • On-page SEO

    • Optimizing the quality and structure of the content on a page. Content quality, keywords, and HTML tags are the key players for on-page SEO.

  • Off-page SEO

    • Getting other sites, and other pages on your site to link to the page you are trying to optimize. Backlinks, internal linking, and reputation are your off-page MVPs.

  • Technical SEO

    • Ensuring that your website is technically sound and optimized for search engine crawling and indexing. Site speed, mobile-friendliness, and secure connections are the main focus areas for technical SEO.

Title Tags

Title tags are HTML elements that specify the title of a webpage. They are displayed on search engine results pages (SERPs) as clickable headlines for a given result. Title tags are important for SEO because they help search engines understand the content of a page and influence click-through rates.

Meta Descriptions

Meta descriptions are HTML attributes that provide a brief summary of a webpage's content. They appear below the title tag on SERPs and can influence click-through rates. While meta descriptions do not directly impact SEO rankings, they play a crucial role in attracting users to click on your link.

Body Content

Body content refers to the main text of a webpage. It should be informative, engaging, and relevant to the target audience. High-quality body content is essential for SEO as it helps search engines understand the context of the page and improves user experience.

  • Thick and Unique content

    • Content that is original and provides value to users.

  • Keyword Density

    • The percentage of times a keyword appears in the content compared to the total word count.

  • Keyword Placement

    • Strategically placing keywords in headings, subheadings, and throughout the content to improve SEO.

  • Content Length

    • The total number of words in a piece of content. Longer content often ranks better in search engines.

  • Content Quality

    • The overall value and relevance of the content to the target audience.
