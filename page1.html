SEO Basics

What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization, SEO is about making improvements to your websiteâ€™s structure and content so its pages can be discovered by people searching for what you have to offer, through search engines.

Important Factors in SEO

  • On-page SEO

    • Optimizing the quality and structure of the content on a page. Content quality, keywords, and HTML tags are the key players for on-page SEO.

  • Off-page SEO

    • Getting other sites, and other pages on your site to link to the page you are trying to optimize. Backlinks, internal linking, and reputation are your off-page MVPs.

  • Technical SEO

    • Ensuring that your website is technically sound and optimized for search engine crawling and indexing. Site speed, mobile-friendliness, and secure connections are the main focus areas for technical SEO.
