Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization and helps search engines understand your website's content and connect it with users by delivering relevant, valuable results based on their search queries.

Key Terms

  • SEO - The process of optimizing your website to rank higher on search engines.
  • Keywords - Words or phrases that users type into search engines.
  • Backlinks - Links from other websites that direct to your site.
